Removing Old Files

You may remove old files that are no longer needed.

Note: Bid receipts for the bid period are also removed when you remove an old bid period file.

To remove old files:
  1. Select the File->Remove Old Files... menu item to bring up the Remove Old Files window.
  2. In the Remove Old Files window, select the files that are no longer needed, then click the Remove button.
  3. In the sheet that appears, confirm that you want to remove the files by clicking the Remove button, or cancel by clicking the Stop button.

Files that are removed are moved to the Trash and are not deleted from your hard drive until you empty the Trash. If you decide that you'd like to keep the files, drag them from the Trash to your home/Library/CrewBid folder.
