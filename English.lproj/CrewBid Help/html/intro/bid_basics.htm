CrewBid Introduction

Bid Basics

Each month you must prepare and submit your bid preferences.

Early in the month, the company publishes line and trip data for the following month. The data is available as paper-copy in the pilot lounge at your crew base and electronically on the company's web site.

The Manual Way

To prepare and submit your bid manually, you perform these steps:

  • examine the company-provided printed or electronic line and trip information;
  • develop a list of line numbers, sorted in order of your preferences; and
  • submit your bid preferences by entering the line numbers, in your preferred order, in the crew scheduling application available on the company's web site.

The CrewBid Way

To prepare and submit your bid using CrewBid, you perform these steps:

  • examine the automatically-downloaded line and trip data in a variety of formats, including line summary, calendar, and individual trip;
  • develop a prioritized list of line numbers by sorting lines according to attributes (pay, days off, etc.), selecting lines that have certain attributes, and manually ordering lines; and
  • submit automatically the entire prioritized list of line numbers to the company.
