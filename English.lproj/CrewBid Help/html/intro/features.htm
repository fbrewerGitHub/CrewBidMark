CrewBid Introduction

CrewBid Features

Here's a brief description of CrewBid’s features.

Automatic Downloading of Bid Data and Submission of Bid Preferences

CrewBid can download bid information (line and trip data) from and submit your bid preferences to the company.

Viewing Line and Trip Information

CrewBid displays bid information in three views:

  • line summary, a list of each line with configurable columns of the line's attributes (number, pay, flight time, etc.);
  • calendar, each day's departure time, overnight city, and arrival time for each trip in a monthly calendar format; and
  • individual trip, a view of each leg of a trip with summary information, similar to a trip sheet.

Ordering Lines

Lines may be placed in order according to your preferences in a variety of ways:

  • manually, by dragging the line to the desired position;
  • sorted by attributes;
  • selected by attributes, with lines that have the desired attributes sorted above those that don't; and
  • sorted by points that are assigned to attributes.

For more information on the views and how to order your bid preferences, see the Making Bid Choices section under Help Topics.
