Viewing Bid Awards

Before you can view bid awards for a bid period, you must first retrieve the awards for that period.

To retrieve bid awards for a bid period:
  1. Open the bid for which you desire to retrieve bid awards.
  2. Select the File->Retrieve Bid Awards... menu item to bring up the credential dialog.
  3. Enter your SWALife User ID and Password, then click the OK button.

After connecting to the SWA server and downloading the bid awards, CrewBid will display the bid awards in a new window.

Once the bid awards for a bid have been retrieved, you can view them at any time by selecting the File->Show Bid Awards menu item.
