Submitting a Bid

CrewBid will automatically connect to the SWA server to submit your bid preferences.

Note: Before submitting a bid, always confirm that you are submitting the bid for the correct employee number. Because any pilot can submit a bid for any other pilot (and, likewise, any flight attendant for any flight attendant), the employee number for whom a bid will be submitted (shown near the top of the Submit Bid dialog) may or not be the same as the SWALife User ID of the person submitting the bid (shown near the bottom of the dialog).

To submit a bid:
  1. Select the File->Submit Bid... menu item to bring up the Submit Bid dialog.
  2. Enter avoidance bids (first officer first round bids) or buddy bids (flight attendant first round bids), if any.
  3. Enter your SWALife User ID and Password, then click the Submit Bid for <employee number> button.

To change the employee number for whom a bid will be submitted, click the Change Employee Number button, which will bring up a dialog in which you can enter the new employee number and select the option to make the change temporary (bid submitted for the employee number for this bid only) or permanent (submitted for this and all future bids).

After connecting to the SWA server and successfully submitting your bid, CrewBid will display an alert that gives you the opportunity to view your bid receipt. It is highly recommended that you view your bid receipt to ensure that your bid was received correctly. If your bid is not successfully submitted, CrewBid display an alert that gives the reason why the bid could not be submitted.

If your bid is not successfully submitted, CrewBid display an alert that gives the reason why the bid could not be submitted.

To view the bid receipt for a particular bid period after submitting a bid, select the File->Show Bid Receipt... menu item, then select the desired bid receipt.
