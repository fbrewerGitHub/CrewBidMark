Error Messages

Could not log on to the SWA server

The most likely causes of an inability to log on to the SWA server are:

  1. You entered your User ID or Password incorrectly, or
  2. Your password has expired.

The corrective actions for an inability to log on to the SWA server are:

  1. Reenter your User ID and/or Password, or
  2. Reset your password. (You may log in to SWALife using your web browser to verify that your password has not expired and to reset your password if necessary.)
