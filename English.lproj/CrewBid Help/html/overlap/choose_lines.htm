Choosing Lines That Do Not Have Overlap

After clicking the Compute Overlap button in the Overlap Tab, lines that have overlap are indicated in the Lines Table with the pay value colored brown, as shown in the partial view of the Lines Table below.

Choosing lines with respect to overlap may be performed in the Select/Points tab of the Choices tab view.

To choose lines with respect to overlap:
  1. Select the Select/Points tab.
  2. Select the Overlap item.
  3. Select the appropriate checkboxes and enter desired values in the points text fields.
