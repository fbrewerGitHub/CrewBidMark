Overlap

Overlap Overview

Note: Overlap features are available for pilot first round bids only.

CrewBid's overlap features allow you to identify those lines in the current bid period that overlap with the previous month's flying schedule. In determining whether a line has an overlap, CrewBid considers the following to be an overlap conflict:

  • total block (flight) time in any seven day period exceeds 30 hours,
  • consecutive work days exceed six without an intervening 24-hour rest period, and
  • the report time of the first trip in the current month is less than nine hours from the release time of the last trip in the previous month.

Because of the many possible solutions, this version of CrewBid does not attempt to resolve an overlap conflict. Instead, CrewBid merely indicates that a line has an overlap conflict by coloring the pay for that line brown in the Lines Table.

The Overlap Tab

Refer to the Overlap Tab below for the discussion of the Overlap features of CrewBid.

In the Overlap Tab, you can:

  • automatically enter trip data from the previous month's bid and line,
  • manually enter additional data for trips that you have picked up and delete trip data for trips you have dropped, and
  • tell CrewBid to compute overlap based on the automatically and manually entered trip data.

You may select lines that do not have overlap and assign points to lines that have overlap in the Select/Points Tab.
