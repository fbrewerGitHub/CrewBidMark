Saving Bid Choices

You may save your choices (sort, select, and points values) for a bid period and apply those choices to a later bid.

To save your choices:
  1. Select the File->Save Choices... menu item to bring up the Save Choices dialog.
  2. In the Save Choices dialog, enter a name for the file, then click the Save button.
To apply your saved choices to the current bid:
  1. Select the File->Apply Choices... menu item to bring up the Apply Choices dialog.
  2. In the Apply Choices dialog, select the desired file, then click the Apply button.

With the exceptions noted below, all select and points checkbox and text field values are saved when you save your choices, and, likewise, those values are entered for the current bid period when you apply the choices.

Entries in the Overlap Tab for the previous month's flying schedule are not saved, as they would not be applicable to the current period; however, the overlap select and points values in the Select/Points Tab are saved.

Selections and points values for days of the month are not saved, as selections and points values for specific dates most likely would not be applicable to the current bid period. However, selections and points values for days of the week are saved and applied to the appropriate days of the month in the current bid period.

Top and bottom freeze values are not saved because there is no way to replicate the sequence of freezing top and bottom lines with the application of selections and sorts.
