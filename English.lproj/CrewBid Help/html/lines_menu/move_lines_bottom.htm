Move Selected Line(s) to Bottom

You can move a line or group of lines into the bottom freeze range. Once moved into the bottom freeze range, the lines will remain in the bottom freeze range until the Lines->Unfreeze Bottom Lines menu item is selected, regardless of sorting or selections.

The Move Selected Lines to Bottom menu item is enabled only when at least one line is selected in the Lines Table. When a single line is selected the Move Selected Line(s) to Bottom menu item changes to Move Selected Line to Bottom. When more than one line is selected in the Lines Table, the Move Selected Line(s) to Bottom menu item changed to Move n Selected Lines to Bottom, where n is the number of selected lines.

To move Reserve or Blank lines to the bottom:
  1. Select, in the Lines Table, the line or group of lines that you’d like freeze at the bottom.
  2. Select the Lines->Move Selected Lines to Bottom menu item.

To restore the moved lines to their normal sort order, select the Lines->Unfreeze Bottom Lines menu item.
