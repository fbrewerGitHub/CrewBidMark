Move Reserve/Blank Lines to Bottom

You can move all Reserve or all Blank lines to the bottom of the sort order. Once moved to the bottom, the Reserve or Blank lines will remain at the bottom regardless of sorting order or selections.

The Move Reserve Lines to Bottom menu item is enabled for pilot bids and flight attendant second round bids only. The Move Blank Lines to Bottom menu item is enabled for pilot bids only.

To move Reserve or Blank lines to the bottom:
  1. Select the Lines->Move Reserve Lines to Bottom or Lines->Move Blank Lines to Bottom menu item.

When the Move Reserve Lines to Bottom or Move Blank Lines to Bottom menu item is selected, the selection is indicated by a checkmark next to the menu item. To restore Reserve or Blank lines to their normal sort order, select the checked menu item.
