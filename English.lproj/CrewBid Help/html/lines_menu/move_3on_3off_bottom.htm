Move 3-On/3-Off Lines to Bottom

You can move all 3-On/3-Off lines to the bottom of the sort order. Once moved to the bottom, the 3-On/3-Off lines will remain at the bottom regardless of sorting order or selections.

A line is considered to be 3-On/3-Off if it consists of only three-day trips, each separated by no more than three days off.

To move 3-On/3-Off lines to the bottom:
  1. Select the Lines->Move 3-On/3-Off Lines to Bottom menu item.

When the 3-On/3-Off Lines to Bottom menu item is selected, the selection is indicated by a checkmark next to the menu item. To restore 3-On/3-Off lines to their normal sort order, select the 3-On/3-Off Lines to Bottom menu item again.
