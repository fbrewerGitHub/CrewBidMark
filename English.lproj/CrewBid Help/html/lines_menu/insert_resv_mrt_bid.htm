Insert Reserve/MRT Bid

You can insert a Reserve Bid or an MRT bid into the Lines Table to indicate a Reserve or MRT bid at that point in the sort order. Once a Reserve or MRT bid is inserted, it may be dragged to a new position just like any other line. When the lines are sorted, a Reserve or MRT maintains the same position in the sort order as before the lines were sorted.

The Insert Reserve Bid and Insert MRT bid menu items are enabled for flight attendant first round bids only.

Inserting a Reserve Bid

To insert a Reserve bid:
  1. Select the line in the Lines Table directly below the last line that you wish to bid.
  2. Select the Lines->Insert Reserve Bid menu.

When the Insert Reserve Bid menu item is selected, a Reserve bid is inserted at the selected line and is indicated by "Resv" in the line number column. When you submit your bid, only the lines up to and including the Reserve bid are submitted.

The Insert Reserve Bid menu item is enabled only when there is a single line selected in the Lines Table.

When a Reserve bid is inserted, the Insert Reserve Bid menu item changes to Remove Reserve Bid and remains enabled whether a line is selected or not. To remove the Reserve Bid, select the Remove Reserve Bid menu item.

Inserting an MRT Bid

To insert an MRT bid:
  1. Select the line in the Lines Table directly below the line where you want to insert the MRT bid.
  2. Select the Lines->Insert MRT Bid menu.

When the Insert MRT Bid menu item is selected, an MRT bid is inserted at the selected line and is indicated by "MRT" in the line number column. When you submit your bid, all lines are submitted (unless there is a Reserve bid, in which case only the lines up to and including the Reserve bid are submitted).

The Insert MRT Bid menu item is enabled only when there is a single line selected in the Lines Table.

When an MRT bid is inserted, the Insert MRT Bid menu item changes to Remove MRT Bid and remains enabled whether a line is selected or not. To remove the MRT Bid, select the Remove MRT Bid menu item.
