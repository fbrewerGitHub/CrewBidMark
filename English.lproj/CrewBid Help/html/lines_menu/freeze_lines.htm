Freeze/Unfreeze Lines at Top/Bottom

You can freeze (lock) lines at the top or bottom of the Lines Table. Once frozen at the top or bottom, the lines will remain in their frozen position regardless of sorting or selections.

Freezing Lines at the Top

To freeze lines at the top:
  1. Select the line in the Lines Table where you’d like to place the top freeze.
  2. Select the Lines->Freeze Top Lines menu item.

When a line is selected in the Lines Table, the Freeze Top Lines menu item changes to Freeze Top n Lines, where n is the number of lines that will frozen at the top.

The Freeze Top Lines menu item is enabled only when at least one line is selected in the Lines Table. If more than one line is selected, the last line selected will determine where the top freeze is placed.

To restore top frozen lines to their normal sort order, select the Unfreeze Top Lines menu item. The Unfreeze Top Lines menu item is enabled only when a top freeze is active.

You can also freeze lines at the top in the Lines Table by holding down the T key while clicking the line where you’d like to plack the top freeze. You can remove the top freeze by holding down the Option and T keys while clicking the last top frozen line.

Freezing Lines at the Bottom

To freeze lines at the bottom:
  1. Select the line in the Lines Table where you’d like to place the bottom freeze.
  2. Select the Lines->Freeze Bottom Lines menu item.

When a line is selected in the Lines Table, the Freeze Bottom Lines menu item changes to Freeze Bottom n Lines, where n is the number of lines that will frozen at the bottom.

The Freeze Bottom Lines menu item is enabled only when at least one line is selected in the Lines Table. If more than one line is selected, the last line selected will determine where the bottom freeze is placed.

To restore bottom frozen lines to their normal sort order, select the Unfreeze Bottom Lines menu item. The Unfreeze Bottom Lines menu item is enabled only when a bottom freeze is active.

You can also freeze lines at the bottom in the Lines Table by holding down the B key while clicking the line where you’d like to plack the bottom freeze. You can remove the bottom freeze by holding down the Option and B keys while clicking the frist bottom frozen line.
