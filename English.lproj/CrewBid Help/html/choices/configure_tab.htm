Configuring the Display

The Configure Tab

Certain features of the Lines Table may be configured in the Configure Tab of the Choices Tab View.

Including Pay with Points

The default is for a line's Points to include only points assigned in the Select/Points Tab of the Choices Tab View. You may include a line's pay in the Points total by selecting the Points for lines include pay checkbox.

Adding/Removing Columns from Lines Table

Columns from the Configure Tab may be moved to the Lines Table by double-clicking the header of the desired column. Columns may be removed from the Lines Table by double-clicking the header of the column. Columns in both displays may be reordered by dragging to the desired position.
