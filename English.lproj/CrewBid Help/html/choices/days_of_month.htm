Selecting Lines and Assigning Points

Days of Month



To select lines by days of the month:
  1. Select the Select/Points tab item in the Choices Tab View.
  2. Select the Days of Month item from the list on the left.
  3. Click on the button(s) that corresponds to the day(s) that you want off.
  4. Select the Select by days of month checkbox.

You may select or deselect all weekdays for the month by clicking the button that corresponds to that weekday. Also, you may quickly enable or disable selection by days of the month, without having to select or deselect every button, by selecting or deselecting the Select by days of month checkbox.



To assign points to days of the month:
  1. Select the Select/Points tab item in the Choices Tab View.
  2. Select the Days of Month item from the list on the left.
  3. Enter the desired points value in the text field under the day button.
  4. Select the Use points for days of month checkbox.

You may assign points to all weekdays for the month by entering a points value under the button that corresponds to that weekday. Also, you may quickly enable or disable points for days of the month, without having to change every points value, by selecting or deselecting the Use points for days of month checkbox.
