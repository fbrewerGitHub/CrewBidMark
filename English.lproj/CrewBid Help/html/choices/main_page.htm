Making Bid Choices

The Main Page

The Main Page opens when data for a bid period is loaded.

Lines Table

The Lines Table displays the lines in sorted order, along with information for attributes of each line. Clicking on a line will select the line and show its trips in the Lines Calendar.

Lines in the Lines Table may be manually sorted by dragging a line (or group of lines) to a new position.

Lines may be frozen in their current position by clicking on a line while holding down the T key (for freezing lines at the top) or B key (for freezing lines at the bottom). Frozen lines are not sorted. The sort order is shown in green for top frozen lines and orange for bottom frozen lines. The top freeze may be removed by holding down the Option-T key and selecting the last top frozen line. Similarly, the bottom freeze may be removed by holding down the Option-B key and selecting the first bottom frozen line.

The Lines Table columns may be moved by dragging a column to a new position. Columns may be added or removed in the Configure Tab of the Choices Tab View.

Lines Calendar

The Lines Calendar displays trips for the line that is selected in the Lines Table. Clicking on the trip text in the Lines Calendar will select all days for that trip and show the trip in the Trip View.

Trip View

The Trip View displays the trip text for the trip selected in the Lines Calendar.

Choices Tab View

The Choices Tab View displays choices that control line selection, assigning points, and configuration of the Lines Table.
