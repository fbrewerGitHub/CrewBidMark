Making Bid Choices

Selecting Lines and Assigning Points

The Select/Points Tab

Lines are selected in the Select/Points Tab of the Choices Tab View. Selected lines are sorted above deselected lines in the Lines Table.

To select lines by a certain attribute, select that attribute in the left column, which will display various selections that can be made for that attribute.

Most selections have a checkbox and a value. The checkbox must be selected for the selection to be effective.

Points are assigned in the Select/Points Tab of the Choices Tab View. Lines may be sorted by Points by selecting Points as one of the sort attributes in the Sort Tab.

To assign points for certain attribute, select that attribute in the left column, which will display various points assignments that can be made for that attribute.

Line Pay may be included in the Points total for a line by selecting the checkbox in the Configure Tab of the Choices Tab View.

Points assignments have a checkbox and a value. The checkbox must be selected for the points assignment to be effective.
